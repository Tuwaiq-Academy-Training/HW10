The best PS4 games for kids



Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy

If you grew up playing Crash Bandicoot games on PlayStation, you’ll be happy to learn that your kids can now do the same on the PS4. (You may also be shocked by the fast and relentless passage of time, so fair warning.) The Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy gathers the first three games in the series in a single collection, adding improved graphics and smoother performance. While the games can be pretty difficult, they’re also bright, colorful and good-natured. They may also give your child a slight edge in Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End, since Crash crops up in a memorable level of that game.





Marvel’s Spider-Man

Marvel’s Spider-Man is cheating a bit, since the game is rated T and definitely has an older audience in mind. However, if you don’t mind some mild violence and language, Spider-Man is probably fine for tweens as well. Even more so than the recent Marvel Cinematic Universe movies,

Spider-Man on PS4 captures the true essence of the character, forcing him to make tough moral choices while protecting the city he loves. The web-slinging feels absolutely perfect, from the daring leaps to the speedy dives, and the combat offers difficulty options to suit every skill level.
